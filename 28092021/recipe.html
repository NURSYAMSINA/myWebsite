Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and tell abount it, and these are their stories.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 stick of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Ler sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy
